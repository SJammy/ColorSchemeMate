Instructions for Use

Choose Colour

Click a Colour Box to open the Colour Picker.

Select the desired colour to test.

Implement Chosen Colour

Press the 'Esc' key, or close the Colour Picker.

Save

Save & Download a PDF with your customised colour scheme's hex values.

Reset

Return to the original colour scheme.

Close

Hide the Colour Scheme section. A button will appear atthe foot of the page to re-open the Colour Scheme section.
